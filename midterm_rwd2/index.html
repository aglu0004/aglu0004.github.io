Silver Controller Invitational

Every year before Christmas, the Canadian Call of Duty League organizes a mid-season tournament to help raise money for local charities to fund individuals who may need supplies, services, and much more. All teams will attend this tournament, and each year a new city is chosen to host it across all three Canadian provinces and territories. As well to balance out each pool, two teams from any province across Canada will be Invited to compete against the best..

This year the tournament will be hosted in Dawson City, Yukon, and will take place December 29th-31st, 2019. Be sure to get tickets when they go on sale December 15th.
